What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device when you visit them. They serve various purposes, such as enabling website functionality, analyzing website performance, and delivering personalized content and advertisements.

Types of cookies used by Do9ni

Do9ni uses the following types of cookies on its platform:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the proper functioning of the website, such as logging in and navigating the platform.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect data on how users interact with the platform to improve usability and functionality.
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver personalized advertisements based on your browsing behavior and preferences.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Used by external providers (e.g., analytics tools or advertisers) to track activity across different websites.

How Do9ni uses cookies

Do9ni uses cookies to:

  • Enable essential platform functionalities
  • Understand how users interact with the platform
  • Provide a personalized user experience
  • Show relevant advertisements
